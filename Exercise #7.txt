--Number Guessing Game --

Goal:
    Store a secret number and let the user guess it.

On terminal result:
---------------------------
Guess the number: 7

Too low.

Guess the number: 15

Too high.

Guess the number: 10

Correct!

---------------------------

What you'll learn:
    - Loops
    - Conditions
    - User interaction

(This is one of the first real "games" many programmers create.)
