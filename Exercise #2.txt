--Simple Calculator--

Goal: Ask for two numbers and display:
- Sum
- Subtraction
- Multiplication
- Division

Example Result:
    Enter first number: 10
    Enter second number: 5

On terminal result:
---------------------------
    Sum: 15
    Subtraction: 5
    Multiplication: 50
    Division: 2
---------------------------

What you'll learn:
- How to perform basic mathematical operations with variables.
