-- Student Grade Calculator --

Goal:
    Ask for:
    - Student name
    - Three grades
    - Calculate the average and display whether the student passed.

Example Result:
---------------------------
    Student name: Milo
    Grade 1: 90
    Grade 2: 85
    Grade 3: 95
    Average: 90

    Status: Passed
---------------------------

What you'll learn:
    - This combines:
        - Input
        - Variables
        - Math
        - Conditions
        - Output

It's one of the first programs that feels like a real application.
