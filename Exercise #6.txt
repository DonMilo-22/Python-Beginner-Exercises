--Multiplication Table--

Goal:
    Ask for a number and display its multiplication table up to 10.
    
On terminal result:
---------------------------
Enter a number: 5

5 x 1 = 5
5 x 2 = 10
5 x 3 = 15
...
5 x 10 = 50

---------------------------

What you'll learn:
    - Combining loops and arithmetic operations.
